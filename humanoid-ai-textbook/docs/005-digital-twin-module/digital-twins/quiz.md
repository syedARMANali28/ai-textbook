# Digital Twins Chapter Quiz

**1. What is the core concept of a Digital Twin?**
   a) A 3D model of a physical object.
   b) A static virtual representation of a system.
   c) A dynamic, continuously updated virtual replica mirroring a physical counterpart.
   d) A software that controls a physical robot.

**2. Which of the following is NOT an advantage of using Digital Twins in robotics?**
   a) Accelerated development and testing.
   b) Increased risk to physical hardware.
   c) Predictive maintenance.
   d) Optimized operations.

**3. How do Digital Twins aid in AI model training for robotics?**
   a) By directly controlling the physical robot during training.
   b) By providing a platform for extensive testing and learning in a virtual environment.
   c) By eliminating the need for any AI algorithms.
   d) By only working with theoretical robot designs.

**4. What is the primary source of data that updates a robot's digital twin?**
   a) Manual input from developers.
   b) Data from sensors attached to the physical robot.
   c) Pre-programmed scenarios only.
   d) Environmental data from external weather services.

---
**Answers:**
1. c)
2. b)
3. b)
4. b)
